1. New user
    1.1 FB
        a. Click on "Connect with facebook"
        b. Accept and proceed on facebook terms
        c. Done
    1.2 Local account
        a. Click on register
        b. Fill registration form
        c. Go to email and click on activation link
        d. Fill login form and login
        e. Done

2. Returning user
    2.1 FB (with account registered via FB)
        a. Click on "Connect with facebook"
        b. Done

    2.2 Local account (with account registered via FB)
        a. fill form and press login
        b. go to email and click on activation link
        c. fill form and press login
        d. click on forgot password
        e. fill email and press send
        f. go to email and click on reset password link
        g. reset password.
        h. fill form and press login

    2.3 FB account (with account registered via Amuzi)
        a. Click on "Connect with facebook"
        b. Done
    2.4 Local account (with account registered via Amuzi)
        a. Fill login form and click on login
        b. Done
